01
Make a sound. Sing, hum, play an instrument, clap, or let the world speak for you.
02
AI listens. It interprets the feeling of your sound and paints an abstract image in response.
03
You respond. Type a single word or feeling. The next round carries it forward.
04
After three rounds, the final image is a duet — neither yours alone nor the machine's.
